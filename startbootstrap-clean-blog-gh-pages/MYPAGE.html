Early Life of Arnold Schwarzenegger

Arnold Schwarzenegger was born and raised in Thal,Austria.

He grew up after the 2nd world war and his family did not have very much.

Arnold saw in a store window a picture of Reg Park who was acting as hercules in a movie.

There was a magazine that had a blueprint of how Reg Park became big and muscular.

Arnold always wanted more than Austria was giving him so he started lifitng weights to become a body builder.

He wanted to use body building in order to help him get into acting and become like Reg Park was in the magazine.

He made it to America and there was a struggle at first because he was alone, had no money, and could not fluently speak English.

He focused on his vision and eventually became the greatest body builder of all time winning many titles.

Actor to Govenor

Arnold made millions of dollars and had created lots of movies, many of which are still viewed today.

Arnold was not satisfied with being the best Bodybuilder or the best actor of all time,

he wanted more so the next step on his way to achieving his vision was becoming the govenor of his home state of California.

Arnold's Successes as a Govenor of California

  • He made a bipartisan agreement to reduce greenhouse gases in California
  • Increased minimum wage and decreased the unemployment rate
  • Major investments in improving California's aging infrastructure through his strategic growth plan




Arnold Today

Arnold still make great contributions in bodybuilding, acting, and government. He truly lived the American Dream

and went from a poor boy in Austria to becoming extremely wealthy and influential all around the world.
